What is Chocolate Cake?

Chocolate cakes is a rich, maist dessert made with cocoa powder ,flour,eggs,butter,and sugar.it's perfect for any occasion,ecpecially for chocolate lovers!




Ingredients

  • 13/4 all-purpose flour
  • 11/2cups sugar
  • 3/4cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 11/2teaspoons baking powder
  • 11/2 teaspoons baking sodav
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup butter milk
  • 1/2cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

Steps to prepare Chocolate Cake

  1. preheat the oven to 350Ù’F (175c).grease and flour tworound cake pans
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour,suger,cocoa powder,baking powder, baking soda
  3. In anther bowl, whisk the eggs, butter milk,oil,and vanilla extract together.
  4. gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients,stirring until smooth.
  5. Slowly add the hot water and mix well.The batter will be thin
  6. pour the batter ino the preapared cake pans and bake30-35 minutes
  7. once done.Let the cakes cool in the pans and for 10 minutes.then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely
  8. frost with your favorit chocolate frosting and serve!
